The Button

Welcome to The button!

Here's a quick explanation of what The button is about:

The Button is a collaborative social experiment based off of r/thebutton, an April Fools' Day prank ran by Reddit administrators. Basically, you can send a message every 6 hours in #the-button. Everyone needs to work together to keep The button going! There is a 60-minute-timer, and when someone sends a message in #the-button, the timer resets. When the timer hits 0, the channel closes and the experiment is ended.

Happy Pressing!
